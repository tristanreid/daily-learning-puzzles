# Long-term roadmap

> **Status: stub.** This will hold the longer-horizon plan for where the lessons go — the
> higher-level arcs and milestones the curriculum serves — to be fleshed out next.

Today the weekly generator follows [`curriculum.md`](curriculum.md) directly: an ordered list of
concepts grouped into Stages 0–8, generated a buffer at a time. That works for near-term ordering but
doesn't capture the *big picture* — the multi-month narrative, which capstones matter, or how the
"train Bend-shaped thinking in stabler languages" throughline resolves.

When we develop this, likely contents:
- **Arcs / milestones** above the stage level (e.g. "reach data-parallel map/reduce/scan fluency",
  "be able to re-derive a sequential algorithm as work/span").
- **Branch points** — optional deep-dives (Lean/proof, Futhark/GPU, interaction nets) and when to offer them.
- **Pacing intent** — where to slow down vs. move fast, and how difficulty should escalate over time.
- How the roadmap feeds `curriculum.md` (the roadmap sets direction; the curriculum enumerates the
  next concrete lessons).

Until then, `curriculum.md` remains the operative source the routine reads.
